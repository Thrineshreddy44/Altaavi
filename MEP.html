About Altaavi

We established our operations in U.A.E in 2022 to offer its services to Construction, Building maintenance and Services industry.. We offer Design, Build and consulting solutions for Industrial, Commercial and residential facilities for all building services activities including but not limited to HVAC Systems, Fire Fighting, Plumbing system, Electrical system works from concept and design to supply and installation all the way down to testing, commissioning. Our projects include for a wide range from Hospitals and Medical Centers, Shopping Malls, Villas, Hotels, Warehouses, Labor camp and multistoried buildings to schools and industrial compounds which have acclaimed appreciations throughout the region.
